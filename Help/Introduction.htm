RAINLENDAR 0.21
 

Rainlendar is a customizable calendar that resides on your desktop and shows the days of the current month. The calendar can use different skins to fit your taste (and theme). You can use different fonts and colors, select the background image or even make the calendar's background transparent. Customization can be done with a simple GUI.

The calendar can also synchronize the events with a server, which will allow you to use it e.g. at home and at work. Rainlendar can also display Outlook's appointments and tasks. There are plenty of other features too. Check the rest of this document for details.

Rainlendar is developed and tested on Windows XP, but it should work on Win98/ME also though Win9x is not officially supported. Also note that some of the skins do not work on Win9x, since it doesn't support transparency. Also some of the plugins do not work corrently with Win9x.
